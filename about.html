About

This is Vasudev Ram's software consulting and training business site.

Vasudev is an independent software consultant, trainer and writer based in Pune, India. Has many years experience in the software field. Has worked on many different languages, technologies and platforms. Job functions done include software development, project management and UNIX/Linux system administration. Former employers include Veritas Software (acquired by Symantec Software in 2004), and Infosys Technologies; (did software development and software project management at both of them). Information about his software, articles, blogs, skills, etc. follows.
